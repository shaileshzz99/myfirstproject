IDEAL THALI

There are 11 different types of ice cream thali available. Modeled on a traditional Thali, this sumptuous spread of Ice Creams is presented to you on a Banana leaf shaped plate.
Each item on the Thali represents a traditional lunch item.

KUNAFA

Knafeh is a traditional Middle Eastern dessert made with spun pastry called kataifi, soaked in a sweet, sugar-based syrup called attar, and typically layered with cheese,
or with other ingredients such as clotted cream, pistachio or nuts, depending on the region..

GOLI SODA

Goli soda is a popular carbonated lemon or orange-flavoured soft drink sold in India since late 19th century
in a distinctly shaped iconic Codd-neck bottle.

BONDA ICECREAM

Bonda Icecream is mixture of natural coconut and add on icream with desired flavour and the combination of both tender coconut and ice cream is called Bonda icecream.
